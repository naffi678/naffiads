What is Network Marketing?

Network marketing is a business model where individuals promote products or services and earn commissions through direct sales and team building. It allows people to build income by creating strong relationships, leadership skills, and financial independence.

Benefits include flexible working hours, low startup cost, personal growth, and unlimited earning potential.
